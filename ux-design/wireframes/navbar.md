## Design Notes

- **Persistent Navigation**: Always visible at top of page
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Most important info on left, actions on right
- **Session Context**: Always show current session/stage when applicable
- **Minimal Clutter**: Keep nav clean, hide less critical items in menus
- **Accessibility**: High contrast, readable fonts, keyboard navigation
