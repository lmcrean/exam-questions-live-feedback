## Key Differentiators

Starter Hero is the only platform that combines all six critical features:
1. **Host your own exam questions** - Create and manage assessment content
2. **Long format answers are allowed** - Support for extended written responses
3. **Instant feedback with AI Marking** - Automated evaluation of student responses. Young people need quick feedback to stay engaged.
4. **Teachers instantly see common misconceptions across their class** - Real-time insights into class-wide understanding gaps
5. **Students see peer responses** - Collaborative learning through peer visibility
6. **Students vote** - Engagement through peer voting mechanisms

## Deep Dive - Starter Hero vs Printed exam papers (done by hand)

By hand:
Pros: quicker, less tech required
Cons: paper printouts requires 3 mins, dependent on teacher's feedback, delayed response, lack of... fun?

Starter hero:
Pros: seeing peer responses adds sense of community and accountability.
Cons: time commitment to set up (3 minutes -- authorise, handwrite, hand type).
